How to Use ▾
  1. 1 Select your Quiz Type — Movies, TV Shows, Video Games, Music, Books, or Board Games.
  2. 2 Click Download Template to get a CSV pre-filled with questions from the 60-question pool for your chosen type. Each quiz randomly selects 20 questions, so you can create multiple different quizzes before repeating. Fill in your correct answers — leave wrong answer columns blank and the AI will generate them, or fill them in yourself. For actor/character answers use the format Character (Actor) and add the show or film name in the Search Hint column.
  3. 3 Click Choose CSV, select your filled-in file, then click Load & Enrich. The app fetches cover art and generates any missing wrong answers automatically.
  4. 4 Scroll down to review the quiz. When it looks good, click Publish & Get Share Link — then copy the link and send it to your friends!
