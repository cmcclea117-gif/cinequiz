How It Works

Three steps to quiz your friends

No accounts needed to take a quiz. Just share the link and let the fun begin.

01
Pick a topic & fill in your answers
Choose from Movies, TV Shows, Video Games, or Music. Answer questions about your favorites — the AI generates the wrong answers automatically.
02
Publish & get a share link
One click publishes your quiz and generates a shareable link. Send it to your friends via text, DM, or wherever you hang out.
03
See who really knows you
Friends take the quiz, get their score, and download a shareable results card. Scores get shared, debates get started.
